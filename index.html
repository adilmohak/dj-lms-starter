Who We Are

Dj LMS is completely open-source with MIT license

Our open-source Learning Management System (LMS) is designed to provide a robust, scalable, and secure platform for educational institutions and developers. Built with a modular architecture, our LMS offers flexibility and customization to meet diverse educational needs. It seamlessly integrates with existing systems, ensuring a smooth and efficient user experience. With a focus on performance and security, our LMS is equipped to handle large-scale deployments while maintaining data integrity and privacy. Comprehensive documentation and a supportive community make it easy for developers to get started and contribute to the project.

If you're a developer, you can...

  • Contribute to the project and upgrade your portfolio.
  • Use the project as a kickstart to build even complex system.
  • Help the project contributors by staring the project.
